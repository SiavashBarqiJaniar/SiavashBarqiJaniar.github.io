Books I have read and recommend!

Deep Learning Book

Authors: Ian Goodfellow, Yoshua Bengio, and Aaron Courville

Publisher: MIT Press

URL (Free): deeplearningbook.org

One of the best books I have ever read about machine learning. There are many practical notes and recommendations in the book that is very helpful when you want to implement ML systems. This book has been cited by the most famous professors and AI engineers. I too have cited this book in almost all of my publications.
